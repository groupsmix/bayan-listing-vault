## Product & Content

### Q: Does the free version of ChatGPT work?

**A:** You need GPT-4 / GPT-4o / GPT-5 / o-series / Claude Sonnet-Opus / or Gemini Pro-Ultra. The free/fast models drop voice quality sharply and the master prompt depends on model depth. Budget ~AED 80/month for ChatGPT Plus or Claude Pro; it pays back in the first week.

---

### Q: Does this replace my brokerage's compliance process?

**A:** No. Every prompt with a listing output leaves a marked slot for RERA permit number, ORN, BRN, Trakheesi (UAE) or REGA license (Saudi). You fill those from your brokerage's live registry. The guide is a writing tool, not a compliance tool.

---

### Q: I work in Qatar / Kuwait / Oman. Will this still work?

**A:** The voice and structure translate fine. The compliance fields (RERA, Trakheesi, REGA) and area examples are specific to UAE and Saudi Arabia. You'd adapt about 20% of the content for a different Gulf market. Worth it, but I'd tell you honestly: the product is built for UAE and Saudi first.

---

### Q: How do I actually use the playbook day-to-day?

**A:** You receive two PDFs in your Gumroad library — one English, one Arabic. The PDFs are structured for copy-paste: every prompt, sequence, and caption is a self-contained block. Open it on iPad or laptop, copy a prompt, paste it into ChatGPT or Claude after the master voice prompt from Appendix A. Most buyers keep the PDF open on a second screen while they work.

---

### Q: What if updates change everything in 6 months?

**A:** 12 months of free updates from the date of purchase. When RERA rules shift, when a new portal replaces Dubizzle, when a major off-plan project changes the Dubai South conversation — the book updates, and every existing buyer gets the new version automatically through their Gumroad library for the duration of the 12-month window.

---

### Q: Is the Arabic edition a direct translation of the English?

**A:** No. The Arabic edition ships a curated core: the master voice prompt (fully translated), all 15 objection scripts, all 10 onboarding emails, plus working samples of listing prompts, follow-up sequences, captions, and market reports. The master prompt's `lang:ar` flag regenerates the rest in Gulf-friendly Modern Standard Arabic on demand. This approach gives you full Arabic coverage without shipping 216 items twice.

## Technical & Setup

### Q: Which AI model should I use?

**A:** ChatGPT Plus (GPT-4o or o-series), Claude Pro (Sonnet or Opus), or Gemini Advanced (Pro-Ultra). The master voice prompt is optimized for these models. Free/fast models (GPT-3.5, Claude Instant, Gemini Flash) will work but drop voice quality by 30–40%.

---

### Q: Do I need to paste the master voice prompt every time?

**A:** Paste it once at the start of a new chat session. Then every prompt in the playbook inherits that voice automatically for the duration of that session. If you start a new chat, paste the master prompt again.

---

### Q: Can I customize the master voice prompt?

**A:** Yes. The master prompt is a template. Replace the placeholder fields (`{{your_market}}`, `{{your_specialty}}`, `{{your_brokerage}}`) with your actual details. The more specific you make it, the better the output.

---

### Q: What if a prompt doesn't work for my specific property?

**A:** Every prompt is a starting point. Adapt the variables (`{{building}}`, `{{price}}`, `{{view}}`) to your property. If the structure doesn't fit, use a different prompt from the same section or combine two prompts. The playbook is a toolkit, not a script.

## Market-Specific

### Q: Are the neighborhood market reports pre-filled with data?

**A:** No. The market reports are fillable templates. You supply the last-90-day stats, comparable sales, and agent commentary. The playbook provides the structure, headers, prose voice, and neighborhood narrative scaffold. You fill in the numbers from DLD, Bayut, or Property Finder.

---

### Q: Do the prompts work for off-plan properties?

**A:** Yes. Section 1 includes 20+ off-plan-specific prompts covering payment plans, handover dates, developer reliability, and off-plan risk objections. The objection scripts also include "handover delay worries" and "off-plan vs ready" comparisons.

---

### Q: What about commercial properties (offices, retail, warehouses)?

**A:** The playbook is optimized for residential (apartments, villas, townhouses, penthouses). Commercial properties are mentioned in the property-type matrix but are not the primary focus. If you work exclusively in commercial, about 60% of the content (follow-up sequences, objection scripts, social captions, master voice prompt) will still apply. The listing prompts will need adaptation.

## Miscellaneous

### Q: Why is it called "Bayan"?

**A:** بيان (bayān) in Arabic means "clarity", "articulation", "statement". It's the root of the verb "to clarify" or "to express clearly". The name reflects the playbook's goal: clear, articulate, human-sounding copy that doesn't smell like AI.
