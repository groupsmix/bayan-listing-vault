## Days 7–10: Public Launch

### Day 7: First Public Post (LinkedIn)

**Goal:** First public announcement. Link in bio, not in post (LinkedIn algorithm penalizes direct links).

**LinkedIn Carousel Post (10 slides):**

**Slide 1 (Cover):**
> I wrote a playbook for AI-driven real-estate copy in UAE and Saudi.
>
> Here's what's inside, and why I think every Gulf agent writing their own listings needs it.

**Slide 2:**
> 100 listing prompts.
> 5 property types × 3 price tiers × 6–7 variants.
> Off-plan. Ready secondary. Villas. Townhouses. Penthouses.

**Slide 3:**
> 30 follow-up sequences.
> Cold leads. Open-house warm leads. Ghosted buyers. Past clients. Sphere-of-influence. Every sequence is 3–5 messages with explicit timing.

**Slide 4:**
> 50 social captions.
> Instagram carousels. Reels hooks. Listing announcements. Educational posts. Personal-brand posts. Every caption includes hashtags, CTA, and a hook that's actually different.

**Slide 5:**
> 10 neighborhood market reports.
> Dubai Marina. Downtown. Palm. Business Bay. JVC. Saadiyat. Olaya. Diriyah. Jeddah Corniche. The Pearl.

**Slide 6:**
> 15 WhatsApp objection scripts.
> "Price too high." "I'll wait." "Mortgage worries." "Off-plan risk." "Another agent showed me this." Text-only, 1–3 messages, with 2 follow-up variations each.

**Slide 7:**
> 1 master voice prompt.
> ~2,000 words. Paste once into ChatGPT/Claude. Trains the model on your voice, your market, your specialty. Every other prompt inherits that voice automatically.

**Slide 8:**
> All bilingual.
> Full English core. Curated Arabic core (objection scripts, onboarding emails, master voice prompt) + on-demand regeneration for the rest via `lang:ar`.

**Slide 9:**
> AED 297. One-time payment.
> 14-day refund. 12 months of free updates.
> Link in bio.

**Slide 10 (CTA):**
> Bayan — The Listing Vault.
> For UAE & Saudi agents who close deals, not write copy like it's 2015.
> Link in bio.

## Days 11–14: Iterate

### Day 11: Analytics Check + Sales Page Rewrite (If Needed)

**Action:**
- Check Gumroad analytics: How many visitors? How many buyers? Conversion rate?
- **If conversion rate <3%:** Rewrite the sales page hero (first 3 paragraphs). Make the pain sharper, the promise clearer.
- **If refund rate >5%:** Audit the PDF. Which section is underperforming? Rewrite the weakest 3 prompts.
- **If conversion rate ≥3% and refund rate <5%:** No changes needed. Proceed to Day 12.

---

### Day 12: FAQ Update
