# Bayan — Launch Ops Kit

## Pricing

| Tier | Launch Price | Regular Price | What's included |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Individual agent (default)** | AED 197 (first 50) | AED 497 / USD 135 | Bilingual PDF (EN + AR) + 12 months of free updates, 1-seat license |
| **Bayan Pro** | — | AED 997 / USD 270 | Standard + 1-hour onboarding call + custom prompts + 3-month Q&A access |
| **Team of 3-10 agents** | — | AED 2,997 / USD 815 | Pro tier × 10 seats, custom brokerage watermark on PDFs |
| **Brokerage (unlimited seats)** | — | AED 9,900 / USD 2,695 | Unlimited seats within one ORN, co-branded cover, quarterly training calls |

**Launch strategy:** First 50 buyers get AED 197 (60% discount). After 50 sales, price increases to AED 497. This creates urgency and rewards early adopters.

**Upsell strategy:** Bayan Pro is offered on the thank-you page after Standard purchase. Target: 10-15% of Standard buyers upgrade to Pro (additional AED 800 revenue per upgrade).

**Team strategy:** Individual tier is the wedge. Do not push "team" upsell in the Gumroad flow — it kills conversion on single-agent buyers. Team inquiries come through email after a single agent converts. Goal: 30 single-seat buyers before first team sale.

## Refund policy

- 14-day, no-questions-asked refund via Gumroad
- Processed within 24 hours of request
- Buyer replies to Gumroad receipt with the word "refund"
- No explanation required; no buyer flag; no follow-up

Target refund rate: <4% on first 100 sales. Above 6%, audit the PDF for a specific section that's underperforming.

## Watermarking and licensing (informational only)

- Every PDF carries a footer reference (`Watermark: see Gumroad receipt`) plus the buyer's Gumroad license key on the cover page.
- The actual per-buyer identity lives on the Gumroad receipt and in the Gumroad license-key system — v1 does not run a server-side stamping pipeline.
- The watermark is **informational, not policing**. It identifies the receipt holder for licensing questions and brokerage-team rollouts; it is not used to chase or punish pirated copies.
- This is consistent with the **14-day no-questions-asked refund**: refunds are not tied to watermark or piracy enforcement. Buyers who request a refund inside the window are refunded without follow-up.

## 14-day launch plan

### Days 1–3 — Pre-launch priming (no product link yet)

- **Day 1:** Post on personal LinkedIn + Instagram: "Spent 6 weeks building a playbook for UAE and Saudi agents on AI listings. Releasing in 10 days. Looking for 10 agents to pilot — free copy in exchange for 1 paragraph of feedback. DM if interested." Capture 10 pilot agents. Send pre-release PDF. Give 72 hours for feedback.
- **Day 2:** Send pre-release PDF to any pilot agents. Ask one question only: "After reading, what's the single prompt you'd use Monday morning? Why?"
- **Day 3:** Collect feedback. Rewrite the weakest 3 prompts based on what the pilot group called out. Ship update to pilots.

### Days 4–6 — Soft launch (off-market DMs only)

- **Day 4:** Gumroad listing goes live but not promoted publicly. Send direct DMs to 50 hand-picked UAE/Saudi agents on Instagram + LinkedIn. Script below.
- **Day 5:** Follow up with anyone who opened the DM but didn't reply — single-line nudge only.
- **Day 6:** Request testimonial from any pilot/buyer who replied positively. 3 testimonials target by Day 10.

### Days 7–10 — Public launch

- **Day 7:** First public post on personal LinkedIn. Post carousel: "I wrote a playbook for AI-driven real-estate copy in UAE and Saudi. Here's what's inside, and why I think every Gulf agent writing their own listings needs it." Link in bio, not in post (link-in-post kills LinkedIn reach).
- **Day 8:** Instagram carousel + Reel. Same content, adapted. Bilingual caption.
- **Day 9:** X/Twitter thread, 10 tweets, showing one prompt + one sample output side-by-side. Link at end.
- **Day 10:** Email to personal network (if you have one) of 200–600 real-estate contacts. 4-sentence email max.

### Days 11–14 — Iterate

- **Day 11:** Check Gumroad analytics. If conversion rate <3%, rewrite the sales page hero. If refund rate >5%, audit PDF.
- **Day 12:** Collect every buyer question into a FAQ update. Ship PDF v1.1 within the day.
- **Day 13:** Reach out personally to each buyer from days 4–10: "How was it? One thing I'd change?" Document responses.
- **Day 14:** Publish a "50 agents bought Bayan in 10 days — here's what they said" post. Include 3 testimonials. Add to landing page.

- **Month 2:** Add 20 more listing prompts covering NEW-to-market areas (Dubai Islands, Saadiyat phase 2, Qiddiya-adjacent Riyadh). Ship as free update.
- **Month 3:** Add "Bayan Live" — monthly 1-hour Zoom where buyers submit their hardest listing and the author writes it live with them. AED 500 bolt-on.

- **Month 5:** Build "Bayan Score" — a free tool that rates a pasted listing copy for AI-tell density and gives a rewrite. Top of funnel.
- **Month 6:** Decide between going deeper (Bayan Pro: CRM integrations, API) vs. going wider (second market). Default: deeper.
