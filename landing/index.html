FAQ

Common questions.

Does the free version of ChatGPT work?

You need GPT-4 / GPT-4o / GPT-5 / o-series, Claude Sonnet-Opus, or Gemini Pro-Ultra. Free/fast models drop voice quality sharply. Budget ~AED 80/month for ChatGPT Plus or Claude Pro; it pays back in the first week.

Does this replace my brokerage's compliance process?

No. Every listing prompt leaves a marked slot for RERA permit number, ORN, BRN, Trakheesi (UAE) or REGA license (Saudi). You fill those from your brokerage's live registry. The guide is a writing tool, not a compliance tool.

I work in Qatar / Kuwait / Oman. Will this still work?

Voice and structure translate fine. Compliance fields and area examples are UAE- and Saudi-specific. You'd adapt ~20% of the content for another Gulf market. Worth it, but honestly, the product is built for UAE and Saudi first.

PDF or Notion — how do I use them?

PDF for reading and reference. Notion for day-to-day copy/paste — every prompt, sequence, and caption is its own Notion block you clip straight into ChatGPT. Most buyers use both.

What about updates?

Free, forever. When RERA rules shift, when a portal changes, when a major off-plan redefines Dubai South — the book updates and every existing buyer gets the new version automatically via Gumroad library.
