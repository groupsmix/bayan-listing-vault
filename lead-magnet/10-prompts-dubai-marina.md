## Prompt 2: Ready 2BR Marina — Sea-Side Stack

**When to use:** 2BR with direct sea view (not marina view).

**Variables:** `{{building}}`, `{{floor}}`, `{{sea_distance_m}}`, `{{price}}`

**Prompt:**
> Write a 105-word description for a ready 2BR in JBR `{{building}}`, floor `{{floor}}`, AED `{{price}}`. The differentiator is sea proximity (`{{sea_distance_m}}` m from lobby to beach edge, measured by foot). Avoid: "stunning", "breathtaking". Add a one-line note on JBR's tower service-charge variability (this tower runs lower than cluster average). End with WhatsApp CTA for title deed and sunset viewing.

**Sample Output:**
> 2BR at Bahar 4, JBR, floor 23, 1,460 sqft, sea-side stack, 110 m from beach edge to lobby door, AED 2,780,000. Two balconies (north Marina, west sea), corner unit. JBR service charges vary widely tower-to-tower; this one runs 21.6 AED/sqft, lower end of cluster. Last three comparable resales: 2.72M, 2.81M, 2.78M. View stack opens to Bluewaters at sunset and JBR walk at night. WhatsApp for title deed and building's OA budget for 2025.
